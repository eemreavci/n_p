Rock & Hunter

Rock & Hunter is an online clothing store which mostly focuses on t-shirts and sweaters with printed designs.

The e-commerce website will be available in Germany, in March 2018.

I've worked as a UI/UX designer in this project together with a full-stack developer. The project is still WIP and some of the product images are placeholders at the moment.

My work included creating the brand look, market research, creating user flows, designing wireframes, high fidelity mockups, banners, HTML emails and providing print designs for the clothing products.
